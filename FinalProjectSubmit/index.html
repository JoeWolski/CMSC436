ThermalViz uses an Android phone and IR sensor to photograph a scene, take its temperature, and generate a heat map.

These images are saved on the phone and can be viewed within the app's photo gallery

This was built using Android Studio and API 23-- however there is backward compatibility down to API 18.

ThermalViz was tested to detect both warm and cold objects (as demonstrated in the video). We also tested to ensure that the items were saved in the correct location, and we were also capable of saving them in the first place. We also tested the app to ensure it works even with the wobbling coming from holding it. Finally, we checked to ensure correct failure patterns if the hardware isn't plugged in.
